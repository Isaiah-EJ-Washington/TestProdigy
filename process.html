Your Path With Prodigy

A structured approach to long-term success.

01
Meet
+

Align goals, expectations, and long-term vision. We sit down to understand who you are, what you want to achieve, and how we can help you get there. This is where the foundation of our partnership is built.

  • Discovery call to understand your aspirations
  • Goal-setting workshop
  • Mutual expectations alignment
  • Initial assessment of your current position
02
Sign
+

Formalize partnership and begin onboarding. We make it official with clear terms and a transparent agreement. You'll receive your welcome kit and be introduced to your dedicated team.

  • Transparent agreement review
  • Welcome kit and onboarding materials
  • Introduction to your dedicated team
  • Access to client portal
03
Strategize
+

Develop personalized career roadmap. Together we create a comprehensive plan that maps out your short-term actions and long-term goals, identifying key milestones along the way.

  • Personalized career roadmap creation
  • Short-term and long-term goal setting
  • Key milestone identification
  • Performance baseline assessment
04
Explore
+

Identify best-fit opportunities and pathways. We research and identify opportunities that align with your goals—whether that's professional leagues, NIL deals, or brand partnerships.

  • Opportunity research and identification
  • NIL and brand partnership exploration
  • Professional pathway analysis
  • Market positioning assessment
05
Target
+

Focus on highest-value options. We narrow down the opportunities to those with the greatest potential for your career growth and long-term value, creating a prioritized target list.

  • Opportunity prioritization matrix
  • Highest-value option selection
  • Risk-reward assessment
  • Target list creation
06
Position
+

Define role, brand, and leverage strategy. We craft your personal brand narrative and position you for maximum impact, ensuring you stand out in your chosen path.

  • Personal brand narrative development
  • Positioning strategy creation
  • Media and marketing preparation
  • Elevator pitch and story refinement
07
Negotiate
+

Structure deals for long-term value. Our team handles negotiations to secure terms that maximize your value—both immediate and long-term—while protecting your interests.

  • Contract negotiation and structuring
  • Value maximization strategy
  • Legal review and protection
  • Term sheet analysis
08
Finalize
+

Confirm opportunity and execute. We finalize all details, ensure all documentation is in order, and execute the agreement with precision and care.

  • Final agreement execution
  • Documentation completion
  • Compliance review
  • Announcement coordination
09
Scale
+

Expand brand, partnerships, and career growth. The journey doesn't end—we continuously work to scale your success, finding new opportunities and building on your momentum.

  • Ongoing career development
  • New opportunity identification
  • Brand expansion strategy
  • Long-term relationship management
